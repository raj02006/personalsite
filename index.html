About me

I will be graduating with a master’s degree in computer science this May and am currently seeking a full-time software engineering position in the SF Bay Area to start after I graduate. I have a BA in Mathematics and a growing interest in data analytics and machine learning. Favorite languages right now are Java and Ruby.

I'm also available for freelance web development projects. I have experience building and deploying production-level sites with Ruby on Rails, Wordpress, Squarespace, Wix, and run-of-the-mill HTML/CSS/JS.

I really enjoy bridging communication between the technical and non-technical sides of a project. I have over eight years of teaching and tutoring experience and take pride in finding the best, most accessible ways to explain a difficult concept.

I'm also the co-founder and CTO of Brioxy. We were recently profiled by WIRED!

I still really like teaching and have helped coordinate a handful of coding workshops for middle and high school students. I’m always looking for more opportunities along those lines. I'm especially passionate about encouraging more girls to get into STEM fields.
